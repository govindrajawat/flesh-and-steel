# 🥩 FLESH & STEEL — VERSION 3.0.0 (THE AAA UPDATE)

![Flesh and Steel Logo](kubejs/assets/flesh_and_steel/textures/logo.png)

**A Brutal Biomechanical Survival Experience for Minecraft 1.20.1 (Forge)**

### 📖 THE WORLD OF THE PERIMETER
One hundred years after "The Great Merger," the world is no longer yours. The ground is toxic, the air is an industrial haze, and the boundary between steel and flesh has dissolved. As a rogue Biomechanist, you must rebuild the factory using the only resources left: Cold Iron and Pulsating Sinew.

---

## 💎 AAA FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS (NEW IN V3)

### 📚 THE ENCYCLOPEDIC QUESTBOOK
We have expanded the questbook into a **massive 300+ quest technical manual**.
*   **Zero-Guesswork Logic**: Every machine sub-system (Create, Biomancy, AE2) has a dedicated, multi-tier progression line.
*   **Deep Lore**: Uncover the "Chronicles of Decay" and learn the history of the Iron Lords through unlockable archive logs.
*   **Stuck-Free Design**: Detailed engineering directives explain 100% of the industrial loops, ensuring you never need to tab out for a wiki.

### 💰 THE BLACK MARKET ECONOMY
*   **The Director's Shop**: A fully functional in-game shop using Numismatics coins.
*   **Skip-the-Grind**: Hard-to-find items like AE2 Processor Presses and high-tier components are now purchasable using coins earned from mercenary bounties.
*   **Reward Redesign**: Completing technical milestones now pays you a "salary" in silver and gold.

### ☢️ IMMERSIVE ATMOSPHERICS
*   **Industrial Haze**: A permanent yellowish-green smog dominates the landscape, turning into a lethal radioactive storm during toxic rain.
*   **Advanced Audio**: High radiation environments now trigger 3D audio effects—experience tinnitus (ear ringing) and muffled surroundings as your geiger counter clicks.
*   **Thermal Panic**: Machines like Steam Engines and Nuclear Reactors emit real heat. Build your factory with internal ventilation or cook alive in your armor.

## 🚀 INSTALLATION & DEPLOYMENT

### Client Setup
1. Download a compatible launcher (Prism, Modrinth, or CurseForge).
2. Import the `flesh-and-steel.mrpack` from the latest release.
3. Allocate at least **6-8GB of RAM**.
4. (Optional) Enable **Oculus Shader Support** for the intended smog visuals.

### Server Setup (Admin)
```bash
./scripts/install.sh  # Installs Forge & accepts EULA
./scripts/start.sh    # Launches with optimized Aikar flags
```
